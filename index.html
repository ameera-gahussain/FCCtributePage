Easy Company 506th Parachute Infantry Regiment

"We few, we happy few, we band of brothers; For he to-day that sheds his blood with me Shall be my brother." - William Shakespeare (from Henry V, spoken by King Henry)

Easy Company Historical Timeline

  • March 1942 - Airborne Command activated at Fort Bragg.
  • July 1942 - 506th Parachute Infantry Regiment activated.
  • August 1942 - 82nd and 101st designated the Army's first airborne divisions.
  • July 1943 - 506th PIR becomes part of the 101st Airborne Division.
  • February 1944 - Mutiny against 1st Lieutenant Herbert Sobel which lead to First Lieutenant Thomas Meehan taking command of Easy Company.
  • June 1944 - D-Day: 82nd and 101st lead the D-Day attack in France. Easy Company played a crucial role in Operation Overlord. (see Brecourt Manor Assault)
  • September 1944 - 82nd and 101st lead the assault into Holland. (see Operation Market Garden)
  • December 1944 - Battle of the Bulge.
  • March 1945 - 17th Airborne Division jumps across the Rhine River.
  • May 1945 - Germany Surrenders, Victory in Europe (VE) Day.
  • August 1945 - Japan Surrenders, Victory in Japan (VJ) Day.
  • November 1945 - E Company and the rest of the 506th PIR were disbanded.
